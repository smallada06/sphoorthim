1. Inspired: How to Create Tech Products 
   Customers Love
   Year: 2008
   Author: Marty Cagan
   Status: Yet to read (0% bar)

   Author: Richard P. Rumelt
   Status: Read (100% bar)

   strategies filled with buzzwords, using

   Year: 1988
   Author: Don Norman
   Status: Yet to read (0% bar)
   Description: A guide on designing 
   intuitive products that prioritize

   cognitive psychology for effortless 
   user experiences.

   Year: 2021
   Author: Teresa Torres
   Status: Yet to read (0% bar)
   Description: A structured approach to 
   product discovery that helps teams 
   balance action with doubt while making 
   customer-focused decisions that create 
   both business and customer value.
